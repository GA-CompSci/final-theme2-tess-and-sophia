Where We Meet

Check the calender for the specific location

Crew 555 is chartered by Dover Congregational Church in Westlake, Ohio but our members are from all across Lake Erie Council. To accommodate schedules, many of the crew and officer meetings are conducted by zoom. In-person meetings and events are held at local metroparks, scout reservations and other area attractions.

VIEW MORE

The Venturing program emphasizes four main areas known as the A-L-P-S model:

A

Adventure

Mentoring, leading, and participating in crew-led adventures.

L

Leadership

Ongoing leadership development through training, mentoring, and hands-on leadership.

P

Personal Growth

Goal-setting in support of personal growth.

S

Service

Leading and participating in community service.
